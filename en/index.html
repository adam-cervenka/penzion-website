Adalbert penzion Čejkovice

Guesthouse and surroundings

Adalbert penzion Čejkovice is a new guesthouse, offering accommodation with boarding, wine tasting and a place for a celebration or teambuilding.

Photos

Accommodation

Double rooms with air conditionning, wi-fi connection, blackout curtains and spacious bathrooms. Parking is for free, bicycles can be stored inside. All indoor rooms are nonsmoking. Thank you for obeying the ban of pets in the guesthouse.

Photos

Food and wine

We serve breakfast with a possibility of additional boarding. We offer wine tasting in the guesthouse or in a family winery, surrounded by vineyards. Our guesthouse is suitable especially for groups of food or wine lovers and also for smaller work events.
